Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 27, 2026

Welcome to Procrastinator, a productivity tool developed by S.Inc. Your privacy is a priority. This policy outlines how we handle data when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection & Authentication

We use Google Firebase for user authentication and secure data synchronization. When you sign in, we may collect your email address and display name to identify your account across devices. This data is never sold or shared with third-party advertisers.

2. AI Content Processing (Gemini AI)

The App utilizes Google Gemini AI to assist with task prioritization and content generation. Any prompts you provide are processed securely and are governed by Google's Enterprise Privacy protections, ensuring your inputs are not used to train global AI models.

3. Security & Integrity

To ensure the safety of your account and our services, we utilize Firebase App Check. This helps prevent unauthorized access and ensures the App's integrity on your device.

4. Local Preferences

The App stores minor configuration data locally, such as your Theme Selection (Indigo, Emerald, Rose, or Cyan) and your onboarding status, to provide a seamless user experience upon returning to the App.

5. Third-Party Services

Procrastinator integrates with the following services:

  • Google Firebase: Analytics, Auth, and App Check.
  • Google Gemini API: AI-driven task logic.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this policy or S.Inc's data practices, please contact our developer support team via the Samsung Galaxy Store portal.
